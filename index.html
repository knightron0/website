About

I am a freshman at Purdue University, intending to major in Computer Science and minor in Bioinformatics and Mathematics. I'm working as an Undergraduate Researcher at the Purdue Heinz Lab, under Prof. Micheal Heinz. I'm looking to graduate through the Machine Intelligence, Systems Software, and the Database and Information Systems tracks.

Currently, my academic interests span personalized healthcare, algorithms, cryptography, and the applications of machine learning in natural science. I'm well versed with app development using Kotlin, NDK C++, or even Swift (for some reason). I enjoy messing around with PyTorch & Keras, and I'm exploring functional programming languages like Lean and OCaml right now. Some of my other interests include birdwatching, Christopher Nolan movies, hotpot, foosball, watching my IPL team (or PL team) lose, and Confucius Say memes.

In high-school, I spent a fair share of my time preparing for & competing in informatics and linguistics olympiads. I represented India at the International Linguistics Olympiad in 2021, and I've attended my country's informatics olympiad camp twice.

Feel free to reach out to me via email; I'm always down to discuss any interesting ideas or problems!
